1643: Torricelli Invents the Barometer

Evangelista Torricelli creates the first mercury barometer, proving air has weight.

1648: Pascal's Experiments

Blaise Pascal demonstrates that atmospheric pressure decreases with altitude.

1843: The Aneroid Barometer

The invention of the aneroid barometer made barometers safer and portable.

20th Century: Barometers in Aviation

Barometers became crucial for altitude measurement in aviation.

21st Century: Digital Barometers

Modern digital barometers are now integrated into smartphones and satellites.
